1 block to Austin's 6th Street entertainment

Hilton Austin is situated adjacent to the Convention Center in downtown Austin, TX. We're a quick walk from exclusive shopping, amazing restaurants and fun live music venues on 6th Street and the surrounding area. Guests of our renovated hotel also enjoy fantastic views of the Capitol of Texas and Lady Bird Lake from 31 stories up. Hotel dining, a full-service spa, VIP perks - we've got you covered for a great stay in Austin.

Highlights

  • Cannon + Belle restaurant, bar and biergarten; 24-hour room service
  • Austin Taco Project restaurant featuring craft beer and fusion taco bar
  • 8th Floor lap pool, whirlpool and sundeck overlooking Austin
  • Spa, sauna and steam room, plus a fully equipped fitness center
  • Flexible meeting spaces, including one of Austin's largest ballrooms
  • 24-hour business center with fax/print/photocopy services

Renovated guest rooms

Modern design meets comfort and style in our renovated guest rooms. Unwind in a sophisticated guest room with space to work and relax. An upgrade to an Executive Room or Suite allows you access to the Executive Lounge, serving free breakfast and refreshments throughout the day.

Pet-friendly

Your four-legged friend is welcome at our hotel. Pets up to 75 lbs. are permitted with a $50 deposit.

Spa at the skyline

Treat yourself at our spa and leave refreshed for your stay in Austin. Our setting on the 8th Floor overlooks downtown - a unique view from the lap pool and whirlpool. Book a deep-tissue massage, nourishing facial, mani/pedi or salon treatment. We also offer spa packages, group events and couples treatments.

Restaurants and lounges

Hilton Austin has a variety of dining options for every palate. With menus and cocktails as ambitious and unexpected as Austin itself, enjoy Tex-Fresh cuisine at Cannon & Belle or go international with tacos and craft beer at Austin Taco Project. For a quick coffee and meal, Starbucks offers all your favorites.

Hotel dining

Weddings and events

From boardroom to grand ballroom, we have 38 perfect spaces for any event. In fact, CVENT called us a Top 100 US Meeting Hotel in 2014. Our meeting rooms can seat 6 to 2,000 guests, and meeting and wedding planners are here to help. Menus, room layouts, A/V equipment - it's your event, your way.
